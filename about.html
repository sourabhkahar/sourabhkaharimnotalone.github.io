WHY WE STARTED

We Facilitate Safe Learning Spaces

Sarve Sukhantu Swaha was founded by Muskesh Ostwal, who tragically lost his father at a young age. This experience made him acutely aware of the immense hardship families face after losing their primary breadwinner. Driven by a desire to help others avoid similar struggles, Muskesh Ostwal established Sarve Sukhantu Swaha to provide comprehensive support to families who have suffered such a loss.

01

Our Mission

Sarve Sukhantu Swaha is a unique NGO with a resolute mission: to offer a helping hand to member families during their most challenging times. We are committed to providing a safety net through various services, including financial aid, educational support, medical assistance, and access to essential resources – all entirely free of donations.

02

Our Vision

Our vision is to empower families who have lost their primary caregiver. We strive to ensure their safety, security, and financial well-being, allowing them to rebuild their lives with dignity and hope.

HOW WE WORK

We take a holistic approach to supporting our member families. Here’s a glimpse into how we work:

  • Financial Security: We provide a substantial monthly stipend to ensure families can meet their basic needs and plan for the future.
  • Medical Aid: Our membership card acts as a lifeline, offering financial support for medical emergencies and minimizing the burden of healthcare costs.
  • Educational Support: We cover the educational expenses of children, ensuring their education continues uninterrupted.
  • Minimizing Expenses: We offer various programs to help families reduce their monthly expenditure on groceries, loans, and electronics.
  • Free Electricity: We provide solar or wind panels to significantly reduce or eliminate electricity bills for our members.
  • Double Your Income: We empower families to become financially independent through skill development programs and income-generating opportunities.
  • Secure Family: We provide a high-tech security system to enhance safety for our members and their families.
  • Employment: Our platform connects employers and job seekers within our member network. We also offer skill development programs to enhance employability.

Fight for You: We advocate for our members’ rights and provide legal assistance when needed.

WHY TRUST US?

Transparency and accountability are paramount to us. Unlike traditional NGOs, we rely solely on strategic investments to fund our services. This ensures the long-term sustainability of our programs and allows us to directly allocate resources to those in need. We are committed to making a positive and lasting impact on the lives of families who have experienced loss.
